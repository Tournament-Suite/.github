# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Tournament Suite is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone — regardless of age, background, experience level, nationality, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Communicate respectfully and constructively
- Welcome diverse perspectives and feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community and platform
- Show empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Publishing private information without consent
- Trolling, inflammatory language, or deliberate intimidation
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@tournamentsuite.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may be temporarily or permanently banned from participation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Tournament Suite community spaces — including this GitHub organization, our developer community, and any official communications channels.
